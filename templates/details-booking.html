Kyoto, Japan

Thu 17 Oct 2024

5 day/s

Wandering through Kyoto felt like stepping into a storybook. I got lost beneath the thousands of red torii gates at Fushimi Inari, and sipping matcha by the peaceful Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) was pure serenity. Don’t miss a stroll along the Philosopher’s Path during cherry blossom season—truly magical!
